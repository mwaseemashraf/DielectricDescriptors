# Literature Index

Papers saved here are grouped by the descriptor families used in the project scripts.

## Ce-centered distance and local Ce/Zr/O structure descriptors

- `Acuna_2014_ZrO2_CeO2_oxygen_sublattice_distortions_local_Zr_order.pdf`
  - Relevant to local Zr/Ce oxide structure, oxygen-sublattice distortion, and EXAFS-based local-order descriptors.
- `Schmitt_2020_defect_structure_chemistry_ceria_solid_solutions.pdf`
  - Review of defect-induced local distortions, short-range associates, and local bonding in ceria solid solutions.
- `Coduri_2018_rare_earth_doped_ceria_structure_properties.pdf`
  - Review connecting doped-ceria structure, defects, spectroscopy, and transport properties.

## Local coordination, site symmetry, and angular distortion descriptors

- `Zimmermann_Jain_2020_local_structure_order_parameters_site_fingerprints.pdf`
  - Local structure order parameters and site fingerprints for coordination environment classification.
- `Cumby_Attfield_2017_ellipsoidal_analysis_coordination_polyhedra.pdf`
  - Quantifies coordination-polyhedra shape and distortion using ellipsoidal analysis.
- `Schmitt_2020_defect_structure_chemistry_ceria_solid_solutions.pdf`
  - Also useful for Ce/O local distortions and short-range defect environments in ceria.

## Ce/Zr occupancy and cluster-correlation descriptors

- `Sanchez_2017_foundations_practical_implementations_cluster_expansion.pdf`
  - Cluster expansion foundations and practical implementation.
- `Zhang_Sluiter_2016_cluster_expansions_thermodynamics_kinetics.pdf`
  - Cluster expansions for substitutional alloys, thermodynamics, and kinetics.
- `Barroso_Luque_Ceder_2024_cluster_decomposition_multicomponent_alloys.pdf`
  - Modern treatment of configurational-energy decomposition in multicomponent alloys.
